## Part 5 — Teacher sustainability

**My between-session preparation time this week:** ______ minutes total / ______ minutes per session average

*§7.2 target: under 30 minutes per session in steady state. If above 30 minutes for more than two weeks in a row, this is a signal that materials need revision — not that the teacher needs to work harder.*

**My energy at the end of the week (1 = exhausted, 5 = energised):** ______

**Am I still doing the 5-second relational check-in during rotation before I ask about task progress?** (Y / N / sometimes)
