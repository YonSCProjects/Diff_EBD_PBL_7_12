Project #1 — Light Signals: Milestone 1 of 5

🏁Start-up — workspace, first LED, first upload

Project 1 • Light Signals
A compressed start-up: set up the workspace, wire one LED, upload the first sketch.
If this is your first session in the workshop — this is a together-milestone. The teacher will walk you through the folder creation. If the teacher is with another student, wait at your workstation. The teacher will come to you.

📋What to do

  • Open File Explorer on Windows.
  • Go to G:\My Drive\Arduino_Projects\.
  • Create or find your folder (together with the teacher if this is your first session).
  • Inside your folder, create a new folder called Project_1_Light_Signals.
  • Call the teacher to open Claude Code and point it at your Project 1 folder.
  • Plug in the Arduino Uno with the USB cable. Confirm the "L" LED on the board is blinking from the factory sketch.
  • Wire ONE LED on the breadboard: long leg → 220 Ω resistor → Arduino pin 9, short leg → GND.
  • Open ino_files/02_blink_external/02_blink_external.ino in the Arduino IDE.
  • Click Upload and confirm the external LED is blinking once per second.
👀What you should see Your folder and Project 1 folder exist on Google Drive. Claude Code is open. The Arduino is plugged in. One LED is wired on pin 9 and blinking once per second. You are ready to make design choices in the next milestone.
✅Done when
  • Your folder and project folder exist
  • One LED wired on pin 9 and blinking
  • Claude Code is open and pointed at your Project 1 folder
🪄Stuck? This milestone compresses several smaller steps into one. If any step feels unclear, it is fine to slow down and do the steps one at a time. Call the teacher — they can give you a more detailed card for any part.
