Pick your pattern

Project 1 • Light Signals
This is your first real design choice in Project 1. You get to pick which light pattern you want to build: alternating, chasing, or breathing. Each one looks and feels different. The choice is yours — there is no "correct" answer. Pick the one you like, and then wire the LEDs you need for it.

Pick ONE of these three patterns

Option A — Alternating pattern (2 LEDs)
What it looks like: two LEDs blinking in opposition — when one is on, the other is off.
What you wire: same as Tier 1 Milestone 5 — add a second LED on pin 10 with its own 220 Ω resistor.
Starter sketch: ino_files/T2_alternating_starter/T2_alternating_starter.ino
Option B — Chasing pattern (3 LEDs)
What it looks like: three LEDs lighting up one after another, like a chase light on a sign.
What you wire: add a second LED on pin 10 AND a third LED on pin 11, each with its own 220 Ω resistor.
Starter sketch: ino_files/T2_chasing_starter/T2_chasing_starter.ino
Option C — Breathing pattern (1 LED, PWM fade)
What it looks like: one LED fades smoothly in and out — it gets brighter, then dimmer, then brighter again, forever.
What you wire: nothing new. Use only the first LED from Milestone 1, on pin 9.
Starter sketch: ino_files/T2_breathing_starter/T2_breathing_starter.ino

What to do

  • Pick A, B, or C. Write your choice here:  
  • Wire any additional LEDs your chosen pattern needs. (Option A: one more LED on pin 10. Option B: two more LEDs on pins 10 and 11. Option C: no new wiring.)
  • Open the starter sketch for your choice in the Arduino IDE.
  • Click Upload and watch.
What you should see The pattern you chose is running on your breadboard. For Option A: two LEDs alternating. For Option B: three LEDs chasing. For Option C: one LED breathing in and out. Take a few seconds to watch it — in the next milestone you will modify it to be faster, slower, or different.
Done when
  • You have written your choice (A, B, or C) on this card
  • The hardware is wired for your chosen pattern
  • The starter sketch is uploaded and running
Stuck? Pattern not running? Check the wiring for your chosen pattern against reference card R1. Option B (chasing) needs THREE LEDs wired, not two. Option C (breathing) uses only the first LED but the sketch uses analogWrite which is different from digitalWrite — if the LED just stays at full brightness, the sketch may have failed to upload. Call the teacher if you need help.
